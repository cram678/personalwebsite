Protecting the Sea Grass

A large part of the aquatic ecosystem in the long island sound is the Sea Grass which provides habitats and helps filter the water, but for a long time now the amount of Sea Grass in the long Island sound has been rapidly decreasing due to bad boating practices and pollution I helped raise awarness of the disappearing Sea Grass by creating an educational video and helped the Sea Grass management coalition with water tests.
